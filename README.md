# Sound Mimicry #

## About ##
Ever feel like your Minecraft world lacks life in the form of sounds, and you don't want animals everywhere, or want sounds you can't normally get at your base? well look no further introducing the brand new Sound Mimicry mod which introduces one new block called the "Sound Emitter" this block requires a redstone signal to play a sound.
